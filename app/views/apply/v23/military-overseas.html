Overseas posting with the armed forces

You may have been awarded National Insurance credits through the Ministry of Defence if your partner was posted overseas by the armed forces.

You must have:

  • gone with them on their overseas posting
  • been their spouse or civil partner at the time
  • lived there with them for at least a week

National Insurance credits can help you qualify for New Style Employment and Support Allowance.
